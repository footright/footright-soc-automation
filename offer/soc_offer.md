# AI-Powered SOC Automation Retainer

## Positioning

We help security teams reduce alert fatigue, accelerate response time, and automate Tier 1 operations using AI-driven workflows that fit enterprise and SMB environments.

## Who This Is For

- Enterprise SOC teams that need faster triage and better automation across existing tools
- Mid-market security teams that need to scale operations without hiring a full additional shift
- SMB and lean IT-security teams that need practical automation without building an in-house SOC platform team

## Problems We Solve

- Too many alerts and not enough analysts
- Slow triage and inconsistent severity decisions
- Repetitive manual response steps across SIEM, EDR, email, and IAM tools
- Poor visibility into MTTR, queue health, and false-positive reduction

## Deliverables

### 1. Alert Triage Automation

- Auto-classify alerts (TP/FP)
- Severity scoring
- Context enrichment (user, asset, IP intel)

### 2. Playbook Automation (Sentinel / Swimlane)

- Build and optimize SOAR playbooks
- Automate repetitive workflows
- Integrate SIEM, EDR, IAM, and threat intelligence feeds

### 3. MTTR Reduction System

- Automated response actions
- Containment workflows
- Decision-support AI

### 4. Continuous Optimization

- Weekly tuning
- Detection improvements
- Reporting dashboards (MTTR, MTTD, alert reduction)

## Packaging

### SMB Launch

- 1 to 2 core alert workflows
- Basic triage logic and severity assignment
- One primary stack integration
- Monthly reporting and tuning
- Best for teams with limited internal security bandwidth

### Growth

- 2 to 4 production workflows
- SIEM plus EDR or email security integrations
- Guided response actions and escalation routing
- Weekly optimization and reporting
- Best for mid-market teams building repeatable security operations

### Enterprise

- Multi-workflow automation program
- Cross-platform integrations (SIEM, SOAR, EDR, IAM, TI feeds)
- Advanced enrichment, analyst decision support, and containment options
- Governance, metrics, and ongoing automation roadmap
- Best for mature SOC teams optimizing scale and consistency

## Outcomes

- 50-80% reduction in alert noise
- 40-70% faster response time
- Reduced analyst burnout
