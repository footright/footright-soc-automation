How It Works

Your alert source in, AI-guided action out.

  1. Your existing alert source detects a suspicious event.
  2. Footright inventories the environment and connects to the available workflow path.
  3. The AI triage layer classifies severity, summarizes evidence, and recommends action.
  4. The output is routed through the client's SOAR, ticketing, chat, or analyst workflow.
