How It Works

Sentinel alert in, enriched decision out.

  1. Microsoft Sentinel detects a suspicious login pattern.
  2. Automation Rule or Logic App sends the alert to the AI Triage Engine.
  3. The AI classifies severity, summarizes evidence, and recommends action.
  4. Swimlane executes the playbook and routes the incident to the right analyst.
