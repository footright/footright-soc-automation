# Sample Case Study

## Reducing Alert Fatigue for a Lean Security Team

### Client Profile

A mid-sized organization with a lean internal security team was struggling to keep up with alert volume across identity, email, and endpoint tooling. Analysts were spending too much time reviewing repetitive alerts and too little time on high-priority incidents.

### Challenge

- High volume of false positives
- Inconsistent triage across analysts
- Slow response to phishing and suspicious login events
- Limited headcount available for 24/7 coverage

### Solution

Footright LLC implemented an AI-powered SOC automation workflow focused on:

- Suspicious login triage
- Phishing alert enrichment and classification
- Guided containment recommendations
- Automated routing into response workflows

### Results

- Alert noise reduced by 65%
- MTTR improved by 50%
- Analyst time spent on repetitive triage materially reduced
- Better consistency in severity assignment and escalation

### Why It Mattered

The client did not need to build a large SOC engineering function to get value. By focusing on a small number of high-friction workflows, Footright LLC helped the team operate faster and more consistently with the tools they already had.
