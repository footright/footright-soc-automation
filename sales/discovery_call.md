## Discovery Questions

1. What does your current SOC or security operations setup look like today?
2. Roughly how many alerts or incidents does the team handle each day or week?
3. Where is the biggest bottleneck right now: triage, investigation, escalation, or containment?
4. Are you tracking MTTR, MTTD, false positives, or analyst workload today?
5. What automation already exists in your environment?
6. Which tools are in scope: SIEM, EDR, email security, IAM, SOAR?
7. Which use cases create the most repetitive analyst work?
8. Are you buying for an enterprise SOC, a lean security team, or an SMB environment?

Goal:
Identify pain, map it to an automation use case, and tie the outcome to alert triage improvement, MTTR reduction, analyst efficiency, and better consistency.

## Qualification Cues

- Enterprise: multi-team workflows, formal SOC metrics, multiple platforms, governance concerns
- SMB: lean team, limited automation, Microsoft-heavy stack, capacity constraints
- High-priority buyer: strong alert volume, visible backlog, repetitive response steps, executive pressure to improve efficiency
