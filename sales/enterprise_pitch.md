# Enterprise Pitch

## Positioning

Footright LLC helps enterprise security teams improve triage consistency, reduce manual analyst effort, and accelerate response time through AI-driven SOC automation integrated into existing security operations tooling.

## Who This Is For

- Enterprise SOC teams managing high alert volume
- Organizations with Microsoft Sentinel, Swimlane, Splunk, or equivalent platforms
- Security leaders under pressure to improve efficiency and metrics
- Teams looking to standardize repetitive Tier 1 processes across analysts and shifts

## Core Message

Enterprise SOCs often already have strong tools, but still struggle with backlog, inconsistency, and manual response effort. Footright adds an AI-powered triage and automation layer that helps teams scale operationally without depending solely on more analyst hiring.

## What Buyers Care About

- MTTR reduction
- Workflow standardization
- Automation across multiple tools and teams
- Better analyst utilization
- Governance, visibility, and measurable outcomes

## Recommended Offer

Start with the `Growth` or `Enterprise` package:

- 2 to 4 initial workflows
- Cross-platform integrations
- Weekly optimization
- Executive reporting on operational impact

## Common Enterprise Use Cases

- Identity compromise triage
- Privileged account anomaly workflows
- Phishing response orchestration
- EDR-driven containment and escalation
- Analyst decision-support for high-volume alert queues

## Sample Talk Track

Most enterprise teams do not need another dashboard. They need repeatable operational lift. We help reduce analyst drag by automating triage, enrichment, and common response paths, while preserving governance and human oversight. That means faster and more consistent decisions across the SOC, with measurable impact on MTTR and queue health.

## Call To Action

The next step is a technical discovery session to identify one or two high-volume workflows that can demonstrate measurable improvement within the first 30 to 45 days.
