## Footright LLC | AI SOC Automation Engagement

### Overview

Footright LLC delivers AI-powered SOC automation to help security teams reduce alert fatigue, improve response speed, and automate repetitive Tier 1 workflows across existing security tools.

### Business Problem

Security teams are overwhelmed by alert volume, repetitive triage tasks, and inconsistent response processes. This slows investigations, increases analyst fatigue, and makes it harder to focus on high-priority incidents.

### Proposed Solution

Footright will implement a focused SOC automation engagement that includes:

- AI-assisted alert triage
- Severity scoring and context enrichment
- 1 to 3 production-ready workflow automations
- Integration with the client's existing security stack
- Ongoing tuning and reporting

### Expected Outcomes

- Reduced false positives and alert noise
- Faster analyst response and escalation
- Improved MTTR and workflow consistency
- Better use of existing security staff

### Recommended Starting Scope

- 3-month engagement
- Initial focus on one or two high-volume alert types
- Weekly optimization and review
- Executive summary of impact metrics
