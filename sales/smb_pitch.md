# SMB Pitch

## Positioning

Footright LLC helps lean security and IT teams reduce alert overload and automate repetitive security operations work without needing to hire a full SOC team first.

## Who This Is For

- SMBs with limited internal security staff
- Microsoft 365 and Microsoft Sentinel environments
- IT teams handling security alongside infrastructure work
- Organizations dealing with repeated phishing, account compromise, and endpoint alerts

## Core Message

Most SMB teams do not have a tooling problem alone. They have a capacity problem. Too many alerts, too much manual review, and not enough time to respond consistently.

Footright solves that by implementing AI-assisted triage and automation for the most repetitive security workflows, so your team can respond faster and focus on real threats.

## What Buyers Care About

- Reducing false positives
- Faster response with limited staff
- Practical automation that works with existing tools
- Better security outcomes without adding immediate headcount

## Recommended Offer

Start with the `SMB Launch` package:

- 1 to 2 automations
- One primary integration
- Monthly tuning
- Fast deployment focused on one high-value workflow

## Common SMB Use Cases

- Phishing response
- Suspicious login triage
- Endpoint isolation recommendation
- High-risk user alert prioritization

## Sample Talk Track

Your team is likely spending too much time looking at alerts that never become incidents, while still worrying about missing the ones that matter. We help fix that by automating the first layer of triage, enriching alerts with context, and routing the right issues faster. The goal is not to replace your people. The goal is to give a small team the operating leverage of a larger one.

## Call To Action

The best next step is a short discovery session to identify the highest-volume manual workflow and turn that into the first automation candidate.
