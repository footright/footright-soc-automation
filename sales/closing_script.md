# Closing Script

Based on what you've shared, the biggest opportunity is reducing manual analyst effort while improving response speed, consistency, and visibility across your security operations process.

Our engagement is designed to do exactly that by automating Tier 1 triage, enriching alerts with context, and orchestrating repeatable response actions in tools your team already uses.

For most clients, the best starting point is a focused 3-month engagement. That gives us enough time to implement production workflows, tune them, and show measurable operational results.

In that period, we focus on:

- High-volume alert triage use cases
- 1 to 3 production-ready automations
- Measurable MTTR and alert-noise reduction

For SMB and lean teams, we can start smaller with a narrower scope and one critical workflow. For enterprise teams, we can expand into multiple systems and governance requirements.

If that aligns with your priorities, the next step is to identify the first workflow we should automate and schedule a technical kickoff.
